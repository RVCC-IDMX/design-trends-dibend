What is Kaspa?

Kaspa is the fastest, open-source, decentralized & fully scalable Layer-1 in the world.
The world's first blockDAG
Kaspa is a proof-of-work cryptocurrency which implements the GHOSTDAG protocol. Unlike traditional blockchains, GHOSTDAG does not orphan blocks created in parallel, but rather allows them to coexist and orders them in consensus.
